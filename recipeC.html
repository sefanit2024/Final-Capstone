Derho(chicken)

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into 12 pieces (representing the 12 apostles)
  • ½ Cup Berberrie
  • ½ Cup Clarified butter
  • ½ Cup Oil
  • 2 Large Onion finely chopped
  • 2-4 cloves Garlic minced
  • 1 TSP Mekelesha Ethiopian mixed spice
  • 1 TSP Salt
  • 4-6 Hard-boiled eggs

Instructions

  • Wash the chicken pieces in water with lemon juice and salt.
  • Peel and finely chop the onions.
  • In a large pot, cook the onions over medium heat without any fat until they are dark and caramelized, which may take about 20 minutes.
  • Add the berberrie. Stirring constantly, adding a little water as needed to prevent sticking. The more you cook, the less spicy the berberrie will be.
  • Add garlic, salt, butter, and mekelesha spice cook until the stew combines. Add some water, depending on the thickness of the sauce you like, and bring it to a boil
  • Peel the boiled eggs. Make slits on each egg and add to the roasted chicken wet/Doro tibs wet.
  • Serve it with injera (Ethiopian sourdough flat bread made from teff) .
    • NOTES
    If you want to do this recipe for younger kids or avoid a hot spice (berberrie), use turmeric instead. Follow the same steps. You just have to use turmeric powder to get it a vibrant yellow food color – Alcha Doro Wet.
