Ades(Red Lentils)


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2-3 tablespoons berbere spice mix
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 3 tablespoons niter kibbeh (Ethiopian spiced clarified butter) or regular butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions:
  1. Rinse the lentils in cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Saut√© the onions in a pot with niter kibbeh on medium heat until they are golden brown.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for a few more minutes until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the berbere spice mix and tomato paste, and cook for another minute.
  5. Add the rinsed lentils to the pot and enough water to cover them by about an inch.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer until the lentils are soft and the stew has thickened, usually about 30 minutes.
  7. Adjust the seasoning with salt, and add more berbere or butter to your taste if desired.
  8. Serve hot with injera (Ethiopian flatbread) or rice
